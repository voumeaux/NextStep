    Communities

    If you're struggling with porn addiction or any other form of addiction, you're not alone. These communities offer support, resources, and connection:

    • Reddit: r/pornfree – A supportive community focused on quitting porn and reclaiming control.
    • Reddit: r/NoFap – A large community dedicated to overcoming porn and masturbation addiction.
    • Reboot Nation – Education and peer support for those recovering from porn addiction.
    • Your Brain Rebalanced – Forums and tools for rebooting your brain and breaking free from porn.
    • SMART Recovery – Science-based support for all types of addiction, including online meetings.
    • 12Step.org – Information and links to 12-step programs for various addictions.
    • Sexaholics Anonymous (SA) – A 12-step fellowship for those seeking recovery from sexual addiction.
    • Reddit: r/addiction – A general support forum for people facing all types of addiction.

    Reaching out is a sign of strength. You're not alone, and help is available.

    About Me

    NextStep was created as a free and accessible tool for individuals (both men and women) who are struggling with porn addiction. Like many others, I sought help through digital platforms and downloaded an app, only to discover that meaningful content was locked behind a paywall. That experience left me feeling discouraged. As a programmer, I realized I had the skills to change that. So I took it upon myself to build something that anyone in need could use without financial barriers. NextStep is my way of giving back a resource designed to empower people on their journey toward recovery and self discipline. This release is a Minimum Viable Project (MVP), meaning it’s just the start of something great. I fully intend to expand and refine NextStep based on feedback and the evolving needs of its users. If you have suggestions, questions, or concerns, I welcome your input. Please feel free to contact me directly at Jelbacc@gmail.com.
